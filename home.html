Government Solar Scheme by Solarica Energy India Pvt. Ltd.

PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

About the Scheme

Launched on 29th February 2024, the PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana aims to promote rooftop solar energy adoption across India. With a total outlay of ₹75,021 crore, the scheme will run until FY 2026–27, enabling every household to generate their own electricity and reduce dependency on the grid.

It is implemented through the National Programme Implementation Agency (NPIA) and respective State Implementation Agencies (SIAs). Solarica Energy India Pvt. Ltd. proudly assists citizens in availing the subsidy and installing efficient rooftop solar systems for sustainable living.

Key Benefits

  • Get up to 300 units of free electricity every month.
  • Central Government Subsidy directly credited to your bank account.
  • Save up to ₹15,000 per year on electricity bills.
  • Contribute towards India's clean energy mission.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen.
  • Must own a residential house with a rooftop suitable for solar installation.
  • Must have a valid electricity connection.
  • Should not have availed any other solar subsidy scheme.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official portal: pmsuryaghar.gov.in
  2. Register using your electricity consumer number and mobile number.
  3. Select Solarica Energy India Pvt. Ltd. as your vendor.
  4. Submit rooftop details and await approval.
